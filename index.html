Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with major international privacy regulations.

GDPR Compliant CCPA Compliant CalOPPA Compliant PIPEDA Compliant Australia Privacy Act Compliant COPPA Safe

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users.

Our application is designed to function entirely on your device without requiring any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our application is safe for children under the age of 13 and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No registration or account creation is required
  • No advertising or behavioral tracking is implemented
  • No third-party services that collect data are integrated
  • All content is age-appropriate and kid-friendly

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete such information.

Regulatory Compliance

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Under CCPA, California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to sell, disclose, or delete. However, if you have any questions about your CCPA rights, please contact us.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - EU/EEA

For users in the European Union and European Economic Area, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • No personal data is collected, thus no data processing occurs
  • No personal data is transferred outside the EU
  • No consent is required as no data is collected
  • You have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing of your data — though no data is stored

California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA)

CalOPPA requires operators of commercial websites and online services to post a privacy policy. This document fulfills that requirement. We do not track users over time and across third-party websites, and we do not respond to Do Not Track signals because no tracking occurs.

Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) - Canada

PIPEDA sets ground rules for how private sector organizations must handle personal information. We comply by not collecting, using, or disclosing any personal information in the course of commercial activities.

Australia's Privacy Act 1988

We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) by not collecting any personal information about individuals. We do not have any data breach notification obligations as no personal information is held.

App Permissions

Our app may request certain permissions solely for local functionality on your device. These permissions are used only to enable features within the app and do not result in any data collection by us:

  • Any permissions requested are used strictly for on-device functionality
  • No permission is used to access, collect, or transmit personal data
  • All data generated through app usage remains on your device

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or social media plugins that collect user data. The app operates independently without external data processing.

Data Security

Even though we do not collect data, we have designed our app with security best practices:

  • No network connections are made for data collection purposes
  • All app functionality is contained within your device
  • The app does not store sensitive information locally

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
